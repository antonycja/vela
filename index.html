THE POWER OF EDUCATION

Education is one of the most powerful tools to ensure people can meaningfully participate in society. It can lift people out of poverty, reduce inequality and ensure sustainable development. Yet, in South Africa millions of people are not able to access quality education. Almost half of all young people in the country are not in employment, education or training, making them vulnerable to lifelong poverty.

Vela Trust is an impact-driven, non-profit trust incubated by the NetEd Group, an investment group combining private higher education institutions to develop the potential of private higher education in Africa. By catalysing new possibilities for higher education and mobilising the needed resources, we are working to open the doors of learning.
